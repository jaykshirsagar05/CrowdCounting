Problem Statement

Crowd counting, which is a space and time function of human sensing, has drawn a lot of attention since it has the potential application in crowd monitoring and scene understanding.

It is a task that seems rather not human solvable. However, a machine can do it. We have to just push the logic, and it pops-up with the precise count.

It helps in understanding the flow of people in various places. The knowledge of the density of people over an area would be helpful in handling emergency situations, efficient allocation of resources in the shopping malls ,etc.
